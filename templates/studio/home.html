COMMON QUESTIONS

Free text-to-speech FAQ

How can I convert text to speech for free?

Paste your script into the editor, select a language and voice, adjust the pace, and click Generate speech. When processing finishes, play or download your audio.

How much text can I turn into voice?

You can convert up to 20,000 characters at once. Longer scripts are intelligently divided and combined into one seamless WAV file.

What languages does the AI voice generator support?

Available languages include US and UK English, Spanish, French, German, Hindi, Brazilian Portuguese, and Mandarin Chinese.

Can I use text to speech for videos and podcasts?

You can create narration and voiceover drafts for videos, podcasts, learning material, presentations, and accessibility projects. Always review the applicable content and voice licensing terms for your intended use.
